Watts

A watt is a unit of power which is energy consumption divided by time



Lumens

Lumens are a measure of brightness, more lumens translates to more brightness



Lumens Per Watt(lm/W)

refers to the energy efficiency of lighting: how much visible light you get for a given amount of electricity.






Color Temperature

Color temperature is a way to describe the light appearance provided by a light bulb. It is measured in degrees of Kelvin (K) on a scale from 1,000 to 10,000. Typically, Kelvin temperatures for home lighting fall somewhere on a scale from 2000K to 6500K
